Welcome to A.N. College, Patna

A.N. College, Patna, is a prestigious educational institution that has been providing quality education for over a century. Established in 1886, the college has a rich history of academic excellence and has played a significant role in shaping the educational landscape of Bihar. The college offers a wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate courses in various disciplines, including arts, science, commerce, and vocational studies. With a dedicated faculty, state-of-the-art facilities, and a vibrant campus life, A.N. College continues to be a beacon of knowledge and learning for students from all walks of life.

The college is committed to fostering a holistic learning environment that encourages intellectual growth, creativity, and social responsibility. It has a strong emphasis on research and innovation, providing students with opportunities to engage in various academic and extracurricular activities. A.N. College also has a rich tradition of cultural and sports events, promoting a well-rounded development for its students. With its commitment to excellence and inclusivity, A.N. College, Patna, remains a preferred choice for students seeking quality education in Bihar.
